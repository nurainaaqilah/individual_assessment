MY EDUCATION JOURNEY

For this page, I will explain my education journey from I was in primary school until today, continuing my Bachelor of Finance.

PRIMARY SCHOOL

After finishing my kindergarten lesson, I continued studying for my primary level education in Sekolah Kebangsaan Machang (1). The school is located at Bandar Machang, 18500 Machang, Kelantan. During my study in this school, I was selected as a librarian prefect in 6th Grade. At the end of my study, I managed to 4'A 1'C for the UPSR.



HIGH SCHOOL

Furthermore, after successfully finishing my primary education level study, I continue my study at my high school in Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Machang. The school is located at Sebelah IPD Machang, Machang, 18500 Machang, Kelantan. During my study in this school, I was selected as a prefect from the 2nd Form until the end of 5th Formm. When i was form 5 i was selected as assistant head prefect. At the end of my study, I managed to 4'A for the SPM.



DIPLOMA

Next, after successfully finished my study at the high school level, I have continued to further my study by taking a Diploma in Banking at Universiti Teknologi MARA Cawangan Kelantan Kampus Machang. During my study in UiTM Machang, I have joined various activities from the first day until the end of the day. In addition, I have managed to finish my Diploma on track, which was 3 years.
